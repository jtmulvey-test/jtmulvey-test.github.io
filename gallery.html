Gallery Controls

<

Collapse the media controls into the top circle.

>

Expand the media controls.

?

Open or close this help panel.

F

Enter or exit fullscreen mode.

H

Enter hide mode. Hide mode starts fullscreen and removes everything except the photograph and image counter.

+

Zoom farther into the current photograph.

−

Zoom out. Returning to the minimum zoom recenters the photograph.

Keyboard and mouse

←

Display the previous photograph.

→

Display the next photograph.

Esc

Exit hide mode, close help, and exit fullscreen.

2×

Double-click a photograph to zoom toward that location. Double-click again to reset the zoom.

Drag

When zoomed in, click and drag to move around the photograph.

Thumb

Select a thumbnail to jump directly to that photograph.
